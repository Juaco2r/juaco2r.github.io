About

What I do

I build practical software that turns microscopy and medical images into analysis-ready data — including registration, segmentation, dataset preparation, and quality control — with an emphasis on reproducibility, scalability, and usability.

Current focus

  • Segmentation modules for quantitative analysis and TME characterization
  • WSI registration for 3D tissue reconstruction and multi-section alignment

Background

  • Design assurance, risk management, and medical device standards

This mix helps me bridge research needs with real-world constraints in clinical and regulated environments.
